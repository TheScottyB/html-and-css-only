Premier
HOME START

We make your path to homeownership easier! Down payment assistance directly from Premier Mortgage Resources.

  • FHA 30-Year Fixed-Rate Mortgage paired with

  • Down Payment Assistance (DPA):

    • 5% assistance
    • 10-year amortized term, fixed rate 2% higher than the first mortgage

  • No required borrower contribution

  • DPA amount is based on purchase price or appraised value, whichever is lower

  • Can be combined with Mortgage Credit Certificates, grants, or other assistance programs

Who Qualifies?

  • Available nationwide (except New York)
  • Purchase only (no refinances)
  • No income limits
  • Minimum 600 FICO score
  • Loan-to-Value (LTV) 90% – 96.5%
  • Eligible properties:
    • Primary residences (1–2 units)
    • Single-family homes, PUDs, townhomes, condos, attached/detached

Premier
VA LOANS

Specialized mortgage options for Veterans, Active Duty Service Members, and eligible spouses.

  • No down payment required

    100% financing available for qualified borrowers

  • No private mortgage insurance (PMI)

    Lower monthly payments compared to conventional loans

  • Competitive interest rates

    Often lower than conventional loan rates

  • Limited closing costs

    VA limits the closing costs veterans can pay

  • No prepayment penalty

    Pay off your loan early with no extra fees

VA Loan Eligibility

  • Veterans with 90+ days of wartime service
  • Veterans with 181+ days of peacetime service
  • Active-duty service members after 90 days
  • National Guard/Reserves with 6+ years
  • Surviving spouses of eligible veterans
  • Required documents:
    • Certificate of Eligibility (COE)
    • DD-214 (for veterans)
    • Statement of Service (for active duty)

Premier
CONVENTIONAL

Flexible mortgage options with competitive rates for well-qualified buyers.

  • Fixed-rate & adjustable-rate options available

  • Down payment options:

    • As low as 3% for first-time homebuyers
    • Standard 5% minimum down payment
    • 20% down to avoid mortgage insurance

  • Loan limits up to $766,550 for single-family homes

    (Higher in designated high-cost areas)

  • PMI can be removed once you reach 20% equity

  • Options for vacation homes and investment properties

Requirements

  • Credit score: 620+ minimum (higher scores qualify for better rates)
  • Debt-to-income ratio typically below 45%
  • Stable employment and income history
  • Clean credit history (limited late payments or collections)
  • Reserves for 2–6 months of mortgage payments
  • Eligible properties:
    • Primary residences (1–4 units)
    • Second homes
    • Investment properties
